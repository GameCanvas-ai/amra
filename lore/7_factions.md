title: The Brotherhood of Light
subTitle: Defenders Against Darkness

callout2: Led by the Grand Master Alistair Dawnbringer, the Brotherhood defends Amra against evil and darkness in all forms.
historyInfo1: The Brotherhood of Light is a holy order dedicated to protecting peoples from evil and fighting against darkness in all its forms. The Brotherhood believes that light is a divine power that can heal evil and guide those who seek the truth. The symbol of the Brotherhood is a white flame surrounded by a golden spiral, representing the light and divine energy of Ethoras. This symbol can be seen on the Brotherhood's banners and the armor of its members.
historyInfo2: The Brotherhood is led by a Council of Light, made up of the order's wisest and most powerful leaders. The Grand Master is the supreme leader, chosen by the Council. The current Grand Master is Alistair Dawnbringer, a brave and wise warrior dedicated to fighting darkness. The Brotherhood holds the Ceremony of Light each year at the summer solstice, where members light a great bonfire and offer prayers to Ethoras. Members process through streets carrying torches and singing hymns of praise.
