historyInfo1: Four centuries ago, the legendary Yaroslav Kievan led war-weary warriors and adventurers to a hidden valley between towering mountains, seeking sanctuary and self-determination. They built a fledgling kingdom surrounded by formidable enemies—Skapta to the north, Aregor to the east, Tianzhu to the south, Banlaya to the west. In those dark hours, House Drakonov, a minor noble family, seized the opportunity to prove their mettle. Through courage and cunning, they rose to power and established a dynasty that would endure for generations.
historyInfo2: Kievan has evolved into a compact, fiercely independent realm where martial discipline runs deep as the mountains themselves. The kingdom transformed its crucible of endless conflict into a source of strength—its citizens are forged warriors, builders, engineers, and scholars dedicated to preserving sovereignty at any cost. Today, under King Alexander Drakonov's steady hand, Kievan stands resolute and prosperous, its people united by a culture that honors sacrifice, loyalty, and unwavering courage.
constructionInfo1: King Alexander Drakonov rules with sagacity and firm resolve, governing through a blend of military tradition and pragmatic counsel. His authority is tempered by respect for the Drakonov legacy and the hard-won wisdom of generations. Grand Duchess Tatiana Drakonova, the king's sister, serves as a renowned scholar and diplomat whose charitable works and advocacy for education have made her beloved among the people. The kingdom balances martial might with intellectual advancement, recognizing that true power lies in both steel and wisdom.
constructionInfo2: Prince Nikolai Drakonov, heir to the throne, has proven himself a skilled warrior and promising leader, earning the respect of soldiers and commoners alike. Lady Elena Drakonov advises on diplomatic and political affairs with ruthless pragmatism. Sergei Drakonov serves as a paladin in the kingdom's defense, combining honor with unquestioning loyalty to the realm.
locationInfo1: Alodon, the capital, rises from the valleys like a stone behemoth—towering fortified walls bristle with watchtowers, and heavy gates stand ready against any invader. Yet within those formidable defenses lies a city of vibrant culture and learning. The central square thrums with merchants hawking their wares, while the royal castle commands the skyline. Universities attract scholars from across the kingdom; the Great Library holds countless tomes on history, philosophy, and war. Theaters showcase music and dance that rival those of any realm. Alodon is both fortress and seat of culture—a city where military discipline and artistic excellence coexist.
locationInfo2: Beyond the capital lie five major cities—Narov, a maritime trading hub where sailors and merchants gather; Mertau, home to the renowned Combat Academy and the Great Library's sister repository; Kolokan, a council-governed center of commerce and innovation; Boritau, where humans and dwarves labor together in mountain forges; and settlements throughout the borderlands, each a small bulwark of Kievan strength.

title: Alodon
group: Cities
historyInfo1: Alodon was founded as the seat of House Drakonov's power and has grown into Kievan's greatest city. It stands as both symbol and substance—a fortress against the world, yet a beacon of knowledge and prosperity. The castle dominates the skyline, residence of the royal family and center of governance. The city's universities, the Combat Academy, and the Great Library make it a destination for scholars, warriors, and artists alike. In times of peace, Alodon celebrates its heritage through festivals honoring the legendary Yaroslav Kievan. In times of conflict, its walls and gates have never fallen.
historyInfo2: Today Alodon thrives as a vibrant metropolis where the clash of pickaxes in workshops blends with scholarly debate in libraries, where soldiers march in discipline alongside performers in theaters. The South Market overflows with exotic goods and, in its shadowed alleys, whispers of forbidden trade. The Watchtower on the city's edge remains perpetually manned, its telescopes scanning distant horizons for threats. Alodon embodies Kievan's core truth: strength, learning, trade, and vigilance walk hand in hand.

title: Narov
group: Cities
historyInfo1: Narov earned its reputation as the "City of Sailors" through centuries of navigating treacherous waters and mastering the Lake of the Swords. The Council of Navigators governs this bustling port, where merchants haggle over prices and sailors prepare vessels for dangerous voyages. The city's reputation for shipbuilding and navigation excellence draws traders from distant kingdoms. Narov's dwarves are committed to maintaining order amid the chaos of a thriving harbor, where both legitimate trade and darker activities flourish. Fresh fish and wood are exported throughout the kingdom; goods from all corners of Amra flow through its markets.
historyInfo2: In Narov, the bustle of trade and the excitement of adventure intertwine. The harbor accommodates vessels of all sizes; the rigging of docked ships echoes with creaks and groans. Taverns and markets overflow with sailors and visitors seeking local delicacies and exotic wares. Yet Narov's crowded streets harbor thieves and smugglers in shadowed alleys. The city's strength lies in its navigation skills, shipbuilding prowess, and unwavering commitment to maintaining order despite the temptations of its own wealth.

title: Mertau
group: Cities
historyInfo1: Mertau stands as a testament to Kievan's commitment to both martial prowess and intellectual achievement. Founded as a military fortress, it has evolved into a beacon of knowledge and learning where the Combat Academy trains warriors and the Great Library preserves the kingdom's scholarly treasures. General Lord Ironshield, the current governor, ensures the city's security while fostering the growth of both martial skill and academic excellence. The city's blacksmiths produce some of Kievan's finest weapons and armor; its scholars rival those of any realm in their breadth of knowledge.
historyInfo2: Mertau's ancient walls stand testament to battles fought and victories won. Yet it is more than a military stronghold—it is a place where warriors hone their minds as well as their blades. The Combat Academy teaches not mere swordwork but strategy, discipline, and the wisdom of ages. The Great Library attracts scholars seeking knowledge of history, philosophy, magic, and the arts of war. Mertau reminds all of Kievan that true strength comes from the marriage of steel and intellect.

title: Kolokan
group: Cities
historyInfo1: Kolokan thrives at the heart of the Lake of the Swords, a bustling center of commerce and innovation where diverse communities work in harmony. The Council, led by Lord Protector Alaric Silverhand, guides the city's development and defense. Kolokan is strategically vital to Kievan—the Association of Gnome Arcanists there has developed the kingdom's most closely guarded secret: gunpowder weapons. These weapons remain hidden, but their existence could elevate Kievan to unprecedented military power in Amra. Trade in weapons, armor, magical artifacts, and lake-based commerce keeps the city thriving.
historyInfo2: Kolokan is a city where cooperation and unity forge strength from diversity. Humans, dwarves, gnomes, and elves work together, creating a cosmopolitan hub of commerce and innovation. The city's market thrums with activity; merchants from across Amra seek its wares. A knightly order dedicated to safeguarding the city maintains vigilant watch over the contentious borderlands separating Kievan and Aregor. Kolokan embodies the kingdom's future—unified in purpose, innovative in spirit, formidable in defense.

title: Boritau
group: Cities
historyInfo1: High in the mountains, humans and dwarves labor side by side in Boritau, extracting precious metals and minerals from the earth and forging them into magnificent weapons and armor. The Mixed Council, led by the Lord of the Forge Thraindor Silverhammer, governs with a blend of human and dwarven perspectives. The city's reputation for exceptional metallurgy and blacksmithing extends across Amra—its crafted goods are sought by nobles and warriors alike. Woodcarving and construction also flourish, creating a unique blend of stone and wood architecture.
historyInfo2: Boritau stands amid peaks of unforgiving beauty, where skilled craftsmen transform raw ore into weapons of legend. The city's inhabitants are proud and fiercely loyal, with deep bonds to the mountain dwarves. Yet rumors persist of unknown dangers lurking in the surrounding mines—mysterious lights glimpsed deep beneath the earth, and whispers of ancient creatures emerging from darkness. Despite these rumors, the people of Boritau remain steadfast and unafraid, determined to continue their work and maintain the city's prosperity.

title: Drakonov Castle
group: Strongholds & Wilds
historyInfo1: Drakonov Castle stands on a rocky peak, its towers reaching toward the clouds. Built centuries ago as the seat of House Drakonov's power, it has never fallen to invader. The fortress is more than the royal residence—it is Kievan's greatest military garrison, home to the kingdom's finest warriors. From the battlements, guards keep watchful vigil over surrounding lands. The castle's armory contains some of the realm's finest weapons and armor, crafted by master smiths. The keep itself, accessible only by winding staircase, towers above the landscape and offers views spanning miles of countryside.

Those who seek entry must prove themselves through grueling trials of strength, endurance, and skill. Inside, experienced warriors teach the art of combat, mastery of varied weapons, and battlefield tactics. title: The Eastern Citadel
group: Strongholds & Wilds
historyInfo1: The Eastern Citadel stands on mountains separating Kievan from restless Aregor, a formidable fortress built centuries ago in response to aggressive raids. Its imposing stone wall stretches endlessly, towering keep pierces the sky, and deep moat surrounds the entire structure. Inside, Kievan's best-trained soldiers stand eternal guard. The citadel houses an exceptional armory where the finest blacksmiths craft weapons and armor of unparalleled quality. Beneath the fortress, dwarven masons carved a labyrinthine network of secret tunnels and passages into the mountain itself—providing hidden exits during siege, continuous supply flow, refuge for intelligence operatives, and hidden armories stocked with weapons for protracted battles.

title: The Dragon's Maw
group: Strongholds & Wilds
historyInfo1: Deep within Kievan's mountains lies a cave that has captivated adventurers for centuries. Legend claims a ferocious dragon once lived here, terrorizing the countryside before a legendary hero vanquished it. Yet tales persist of untold wealth hidden within the cave's twisting passages. The journey is perilous—travelers must trek through treacherous terrain, climb steep cliffs, and navigate narrow tunnels behind a hidden waterfall. Inside awaits a labyrinth of passages with no clear path, traps, hidden crevices, and deadly monsters. Treasures of legend fill the depths—ancient artifacts, magical weapons, and piles of gold. For some Kievan clans, the Dragon's Maw is sacred ground where the dragon is revered as a symbol of strength and wisdom.

title: The South Market
group: Strongholds & Wilds
historyInfo1: The South Market of Alodon is adventure incarnate—labyrinthine alleys, haphazard stalls, and countless wares create a hub of ceaseless activity. Exotic spices, fine textiles, and freshly cooked foods mingle their aromas with the sounds of haggling merchants and traders announcing their wares. Beneath the bright facade, however, a darker underbelly lurks. Illicit goods flow through shadowed corners—dangerous alchemical substances, forbidden drugs, black powder. Possession carries swift and severe punishment, yet unscrupulous merchants provide these items to those with coin and desperation enough to risk discovery.

title: The Watchtower
group: Strongholds & Wilds
historyInfo1: The Watchtower stands tall on a hill overlooking Kievan's borderlands, a symbol of the kingdom's commitment to protecting its people and lands. Its narrow, imposing design houses the latest defensive and surveillance technologies—telescopes to spot distant danger, short-range guns to repel invaders, detection devices. Beyond its defensive capabilities, the Watchtower holds great significance as a meeting place for kingdom leaders, where important decisions are made and traditions honored. Its interior displays murals and tapestries depicting the kingdom's history and heroes; ancient battle relics are proudly shown. From the tower's peak, one can see mountains, valleys, forests, and on clear days, the distant sea.

title: The Wolf's Pass
group: Strongholds & Wilds
historyInfo1: Wolf's Pass winds through mountains, surrounded by steep cliffs and perilous gorges—a key strategic location for Kievan's defense. The pass is fortified with formidable forts and checkpoints manned by experienced warriors. It has been the site of countless historical battles where legendary heroes and villains met their fate. The blood of brave warriors is said to have soaked into the pass's very rocks and soil, imbuing it with dark and foreboding power. Despite the dangers, adventurers seek passage, drawn by promises of riches and the thrill of perilous adventure. Many have perished; others have emerged victorious with pockets full of gold and tales of bravery and daring.
