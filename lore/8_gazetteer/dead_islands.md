title: The Dead Islands
subTitle: Cursed Realm of Eternal Darkness
pullQuote1Top: Undead dominion
pullQuote1Bottom: shrouded in shadow
callout1: The Dead Islands are ruled by Naharazul the Lich, a being of immense power who was once a mortal wizard obsessed with immortality.
callout2: A powerful magical barrier confines the islands' horrors, yet many fear the day Naharazul's ambitions turn toward the mainland.
historyInfo1: Over a millennium ago, a renowned human wizard named Narinthal became consumed by the desire for immortality as age took its toll. He devoted himself to the dark art of necromancy, journeying across the world seeking hidden relics and forbidden knowledge. At last, Narinthal discovered the ancient grimoire "Book of Eternal Shadows," which contained the ritual to transform oneself into a lich—an undead being of immense power and endless existence. Drawn by the promise of eternal life and unending arcane study, Narinthal crafted the soul-vessel known as the Philotrapax and performed the dark ritual, forsaking his humanity to become Naharazul the Lich.
historyInfo2: With the passage of time, Naharazul gathered power and knowledge, attracting legions of demonic and undead beings to his dominion. He chose the Dead Islands as his sanctuary, a perfect haven for his nefarious studies, and there established a fortress of darkness populated by countless undead servants. Naharazul erected a powerful magical barrier that confines his vile minions to the islands and shields his activities from the scrutiny of other great powers. As his might grows, so does the dread that one day his thirst for knowledge and power will be sated by these islands alone, and he will turn his malevolent attention toward the mainland to conquer new realms.
constructionInfo1: The Dead Islands are governed absolutely by Naharazul the Lich, a single ruler of immense supernatural power who brooks no challenge to his authority. The islands have no other formal government—they are simply a domain of darkness where the undead and demonic servants obey their master's will without question. The barrier that surrounds the islands serves not only to contain the abominations within but also to project Naharazul's power and shield his studies from external interference. Some scholars believe the barrier itself is sentient, responsive to the lich's will and hostile to any who would breach it.
constructionInfo2: Key figures in the Dead Islands' shadow include Naharazul the Lich (supreme ruler, ancient and malevolent), lesser lich lords who serve as his lieutenants, ancient vampires who command hordes of undead, and powerful demons bound to his service. Beyond the islands, those most concerned with the Dead Islands include Lord Gavric Rockhammer (leader of Dhon Mendor, seeking allies to launch expeditions against the islands), Master Craftswoman Freyja Ironheart (master craftsman of Dhon Vondaral, studying magical artifacts recovered from the islands), and Selene Nighteye (elf explorer dedicated to discovering the secrets of the islands and finding a way to eradicate the evil dwelling there).
locationInfo1: The Dead Islands themselves are a grim and desolate archipelago shrouded in perpetual gloom and magical mist. The islands' terrain is barren and twisted, with skeletal trees, crumbling stone structures of unknown age, and fortifications built by Naharazul to house his armies. At the islands' heart stands the Obsidian Citadel, Naharazul's fortress—a towering structure of black stone and dark magic from which the lich commands his domain. The islands' shores are ringed with undead guardians and demonic sentries, making approach treacherous. The very air seems to drain hope and life force from any who linger.
locationInfo2: Beyond Naharazul's immediate domain, the Dead Islands contain numerous sites of interest and danger: ancient ruins predating even the lich's arrival, shrouded temples where dark rituals are conducted, necromantic laboratories where new undead are created, crypts holding the remains of centuries of the fallen, and locations of immense magical power where the barrier between life and death grows thin. Dwarves of Dhon Mendor and Dhon Vondaral have explored portions of the islands, returning with tales of horrors and occasional artifacts of power, yet each expedition claims more lives and yields fewer answers.

title: Naharazul the Lich
historyInfo1: Naharazul was once Narinthal, a human wizard of formidable talent and ambition. Driven by an insatiable hunger for knowledge and power, and facing the inevitable decline of mortal age, Narinthal became obsessed with transcending death itself. He devoted his entire existence to the study of necromancy, learning forbidden arts that transformed him from scholar into something far darker and more terrible.
historyInfo2: Through dark ritual and unspeakable sacrifice, Narinthal completed his transformation into Naharazul the Lich, trading his mortality for eternal undeath and power beyond mortal comprehension. Now, over a thousand years later, Naharazul remains—ancient beyond measure, terrible beyond imagination, ruling the Dead Islands with absolute authority. Whether his ambitions remain confined to the islands or whether he merely bides his time awaiting an opportunity to expand his dominion toward the mainland remains one of the greatest mysteries—and greatest fears—of the civilized world.

title: Obsidian Citadel
historyInfo1: The Obsidian Citadel stands at the heart of the Dead Islands, a fortress of black stone and dark magic built to house Naharazul's throne and serve as the command center for his armies of undead. The citadel is surrounded by walls of blackened granite and towers of obsidian that seem to absorb light and hope alike. Within its depths, Naharazul conducts his studies and schemes, slowly accumulating power and knowledge.
historyInfo2: The citadel is impregnable to conventional assault, protected by magical wards of immense power, armies of undead, and demons bound to Naharazul's service. The few explorers and adventurers who have glimpsed it from afar report a sense of overwhelming dread and malevolence emanating from the structure itself. The citadel represents both Naharazul's power and his ambition—a monument to his dominion over death itself.

title: Necromantic Laboratories
historyInfo1: Throughout the Dead Islands, Naharazul maintains laboratories devoted to the study and creation of undead. Here, necromantic rituals are performed to raise new servitors, and experiments are conducted to enhance the powers of existing undead creatures. The laboratories are places of horror, filled with the remnants of failed experiments and the sounds of suffering.
historyInfo2: These laboratories are heavily guarded and largely inaccessible to the living, yet their existence is known through accounts of those rare explorers who have discovered evidence of their work. Some scholars believe the laboratories contain knowledge that could be invaluable for combating Naharazul's influence, though the cost of obtaining such knowledge would be considerable.
